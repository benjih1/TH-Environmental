Biography

***Unfortunately due to current project commitments the pond health check service will not be available until early 2021***

A graduate in Zoological Sciences, I have spent the last 17 years working within the water industry where I have gained a plethora of experience in this field. Specialising as a microbiologist, I worked for seven years in a UKAS accredited water quality laboratory. Here I developed skills in; accurate lab techniques, aseptic techniques and microscopy.

Working for eight years as a senior process scientist within the Process Technology team, I developed skills in safe and accurate catchment sampling. This included multi source, biological, chemical, wastewater, drinking water and catchment sampling. Furthermore, developing best practice methodology for bacterial sampling in river catchments provided a wealth of experience. It was a pleasure to be involved in cutting edge innovative projects- including the use of green technologies to meet regulatory requirements.

Recently, the last two years have afforded me the opportunity to work as the Catchment Technical Specialist for the Integrated Catchment team. Here, I helped to drive forward technical aspects based on clients’ goals and ambitions to improve the river catchments within a geographical area. I worked closely with many stakeholders including; local authorities, Rivers Trusts, environmental groups and farming communities. Developing and instigating catchment projects such as; nutrient balancing models, green solutions and partnerships were projects that afforded me the opportunity to gain significant experience.

At present, I’m on a short-term contract with Natural England as a Lead Advisor for water quality where I have improved my knowledge and skills in: catchment sensitive farming; farming rules for water, and protected sites.

Pond Health Checks

Sample and accurately detect the presence of nutrient contaminants on site. No delays offering resolutions to water quality and fish health problems.

Water Quality Sampling

I offer a comprehensive list of chemical and biological tests, as well as expert safe and clean sampling practices in line with regulatory requirements. Ideal for open water swimming events, inland diving centres or any recreational water body.

Wastewater Process

Testing and works performance optimisation for both chemical and biological processes - both on private (septic tank) or industrial scale. Capacity to sample and analyse a full suite of chemical and biological tests with 10 years of site experience.

River Catchments

Consultation and advice on all aspects of sampling, analysing and improving river environments and their catchments, specialising in point source pollution identification and mitigation.
