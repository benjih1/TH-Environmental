Biography




I have worked as a Catchment Technical Specialist for a water company in the last few years, driving forward technical aspects according to clients’ goals and ambitions to improve river catchments within a geographical area.

This allowed me the opportunity to work closely with a wide range of stakeholders including: local authorities, NGOs such as the Rivers Trusts, environmental groups, academia and farming communities.

In my role, I developed and delivered catchment management projects that involved: nutrient balancing models, nature-based solutions, natural capital and partnership working, which allowed me to develop significant experience in these types of initiatives.

At present, I’m on a short-term contract with Natural England as a Lead Advisor for water quality, where I continue to develop my knowledge and skills in: catchment sensitive farming; farming rules for water and protected sites.

Please see below for more information on services offered. If you would like more information, please do not hesitate to contact me.

Pond Health Checks

Sample and accurately detect the presence of nutrient contaminants on site. No delays offering resolutions to water quality and fish health problems.

Water Quality Sampling

I offer a comprehensive list of chemical and biological tests, as well as expert safe and clean sampling practices in line with regulatory requirements. Ideal for open water swimming events, inland diving centres or any recreational water body.

Wastewater Process

Testing and works performance optimisation for both chemical and biological processes - both on private (septic tank) or industrial scale. Capacity to sample and analyse a full suite of chemical and biological tests with 10 years of site experience.

River Catchments

Consultation and advice on all aspects of sampling, analysing and improving river environments and their catchments, specialising in point source pollution identification and mitigation.
