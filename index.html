Biography

A graduate in Zoological Sciences I have spent the last 17 years working within the water industry, my first 7 years were spent in the Laboratory services a UKAS accredited water quality Lab within the Life sciences team specialising as a microbiologist where I developed skills in accurate lab techniques, Aseptic techniques and Microscopy.

My next 8 years were spent as a Senior Process Scientist within the Process Technology Team developing skills in safe and accurate multi source biological, chemical, wastewater, drinking water and catchment sampling. Including developing a best practice methodology for bacterial sampling in river catchments. I was involved in cutting edge innovative projects including the use of green technologies to meet regulatory requirements.

My last 2 years were spent as the Catchment Technical specialist for the integrated catchment team helping to drive forward from a technical aspect their goals and ambitions to improve the river catchments within a geographical area. I worked closely with many stakeholders including local authorities, Rivers Trusts, Environmental groups and Farming communities. I developed and instigated catchment projects, Nutrient balancing models, green solutions and partnerships
